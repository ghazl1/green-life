Drone for farming

Drone farming

The drone starts by scanning the terrain to create a 3D map. The most efficient farming pattern for the area is then calculated using algorithms. The plane loaded with the germinated seeds then shoots the seeds onto the ground at a rate of seeds per second, about 100,000 per day. This number can be raised to 1 billion trees per year using an aircraft team of 60 drones.
